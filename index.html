Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about your visit to Aura Wellness Spa. Can't find the answer you're looking for? Reach out to our friendly team.

Booking is easy! You can reserve your treatment through our online booking system, call us directly, or send a WhatsApp message. We recommend booking at least 48 hours in advance to secure your preferred time slot.

We provide plush robes, slippers, and disposable undergarments for all treatments. Wear whatever makes you comfortable — many guests arrive in casual clothing and change upon arrival. For massages, you'll be professionally draped at all times.

Absolutely. We understand plans change. You can reschedule or cancel free of charge up to 24 hours before your appointment. Cancellations within 24 hours may incur a 50% fee. Just call us or use the link in your confirmation email.

Yes! Our gift cards make the perfect present for any occasion. Choose from specific treatments or monetary values. Gift cards are valid for 12 months and can be purchased online or at our reception. We also offer beautifully packaged physical cards.

We recommend starting with our Signature Massage or Classic Facial — both are gentle, customizable, and perfect for first-timers. Our therapists will consult with you before the treatment to tailor the experience to your comfort level and needs.

We recommend arriving 15–20 minutes early to complete any paperwork, change into your robe, and enjoy a complimentary herbal tea in our relaxation lounge. This ensures you get the full benefit of your treatment time without feeling rushed.

Yes, we exclusively use premium organic, cruelty-free, and sustainably sourced products. We partner with trusted brands that align with our wellness philosophy. If you have allergies or sensitivities, please inform us when booking so we can prepare accordingly.

Yes, we offer complimentary secure parking for all our guests. Upon arrival, our concierge will assist you. We also provide valet service during peak hours. For those using ride-sharing services, simply mention "Aura Wellness Spa" and drivers will know the drop-off point.
